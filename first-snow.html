My First Snow Day!

March 21, 2014

I'm so happy I have my momma!
It is so cold and wet outside! I don't know what to do about it!
Momma said it should be the last snow. I hope so! If I never see this snow stuff again, it will be too soon!
The other doggies in the neighborhood seem to really enjoy it. I can hear them playing as Momma is carrying me in her arms. She bundled me up in her sweater, so I'm nice and warm. I never want to leave the sweater!
There were some areas where the snow wasn't very deep. That was pretty nice actually. I could see Momma and the house! The rest of world was cut off by snow walls though.
Maybe when I'm bigger I'll like snow, but for now I just want it to go away!
